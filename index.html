01 — The Challenge

You have powerful healing tools.
But no scalable way to sell them.

In Plain English

You want to sell your self-hypnosis cards — but printing, shipping, and storage aren't an option. And a plain PDF download feels cheap. We need something that feels like a real product without the cost of making a real product.

Natasha, you're already guiding clients through transformative self-hypnosis work. The next step — turning that expertise into a product — hits a wall the moment physical inventory enters the picture. Printing, fulfillment, storage, shipping… none of that aligns with the freedom-first model Soul Healing is built on.

At the same time, a simple PDF undervalues what you've created. People don't cherish a PDF. They don't return to it. They don't share it. And it does nothing to communicate the premium, transformative nature of your practice.

The Problem

The Current Gap

Physical card decks require inventory, upfront production, shipping, and fulfillment — none of which scale with a solo wellness practice.

Static PDFs and printables flood the market at $3–$12, which erodes perceived value and attracts bargain shoppers instead of committed clients.

There's currently no branded, interactive, premium digital experience that represents Soul Healing's unique approach.

The Solution

Our Proposed Answer

A custom-built, branded web app that delivers 20 self-hypnosis technique cards as a living, interactive, holographic digital experience — with zero inventory and zero overhead.

Customers buy a unique access key, unlock their deck in a browser or installed app, and experience cards that tilt, shimmer, flip, and respond to their touch in real time.

The product feels premium because it is premium — and can be priced accordingly.

02 — Customer Journey

From purchase to pocket —
every step of the experience

In Plain English

A customer buys the deck, gets an email with their personal key, opens a web link, enters the key, and the cards are theirs — on any phone or computer, forever. No app store. No account. No password.

The flow was designed to feel effortless for the customer and zero-maintenance for you. Here's every step from the moment they click "buy" to the moment their deck is installed on their phone.

01
Purchase

Customer buys the deck

They buy through your existing platform (Wix store, Gumroad, or Payhip). After payment, they automatically get an email with a unique access key tied to their purchase.

Like getting a license code for a piece of software — it belongs only to them.
02
Access

They visit the Soul Healing Card App

The email has a link to the branded web app. They enter their key into a clean, elegant unlock screen. No password. No account creation. Just the key.

The simplest possible entry — like opening a magical envelope.
03
Agreement

Terms accepted on first unlock

A one-time pop-up appears: for personal use only, not for resale, watermarked to their key. They tap "I Agree, Enter My Deck". Never appears again after the first visit.

Protects your intellectual property the moment they enter.
04
Dashboard

Their deck dashboard loads

A beautifully branded home screen greets them with a thumbnail of the Soul Healing deck, the pack name, and the date they bought it. One clear button: "Open My Deck."

Their personal library — ready for future decks to be added later.
05
Experience

The 3D holographic experience begins

Cards appear in a stacked fan. They tilt in 3D with the mouse or phone tilt. A shimmer layer catches the light. They can flip cards, click through, shuffle, reset, and browse the full gallery. Every movement feels intentional.

This is the "wow" moment — screenshots of this will sell the product on social.
06
Download

They download what they need

Any card downloads as a high-resolution PNG — front only, back only, or both. The full deck downloads as a branded PDF Ebook with a cover page, your intro, and every card formatted for reading.

Three download options mean the cards travel with them everywhere — even offline.
07
Install

They add the app to their phone

A subtle prompt lets them add the Soul Healing app to their home screen — works on iPhone and Android, no App Store involved. Built-in instructions walk them through it. Once installed, the app opens like any other app, even without internet.

No Apple or Google cut — you keep 100% of your revenue.

08 — Next Steps

What happens
after you say yes

In Plain English

Five clear phases. You approve the look, send us the content and art, and we build. Then you test it, we polish it, and it goes live for sale.

  • 1 Brand Direction Session — We lock in Soul Healing's visual mood, color palette, and font direction for the app and card designs. Roughly one week.
  • 2 Content Delivery — You provide the 20 technique names, descriptions, and the Ebook introduction text. Usually the longest step and happens in parallel with card design.
  • 3 Card Design — 40 card PNGs (20 fronts + 20 backs) designed in Canva or Figma using the approved template. Thiink design support available as an add-on.
  • 4 App Build — Thiink Media builds the full web app, integrates the cards, configures the key system, and connects it to your payment platform. Roughly 2–3 weeks.
  • 5 Review & Launch — You test the full experience on phone and desktop. We make final refinements. The product goes live for sale.
